QUEEN TRIBUTE PAGE

Biography:

In the 1970's The British rock band Queen was born. The lead vocalist and pianist was Freddie Mercury. Brian May played the guitar, the drummer was Roger Taylor and Bass player was John Deacon. Queen had fifty three top hits until the lead vocalist, Freddie Mercury, died in 1991. Six of those hits were number one in the charts. The Queen released a total of 188 songs during the life of the band.
  • In 2001 Queen was registered in the Hall of Fame.
  • In 2002 Queen got a star on the hollywood walk of fame.
  • In 2004 Band members, Brian May and Roger Taylor along with Paul Rodgers (new lead singer) tried to revive Queen.
  • The rock band, Queen, was dispersed in 2009.
